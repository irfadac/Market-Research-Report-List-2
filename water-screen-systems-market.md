<p><p>Water screen systems are used in various industries for screening debris from water sources. Traveling water screen systems use a rotating belt to remove debris, while static water screen systems use stationary screens for the same purpose. Traveling water screen systems are ideal for high-flow applications where continuous debris removal is needed, while static water screen systems are suitable for lower-flow applications with periodic cleaning requirements. Both types of systems play a crucial role in maintaining the efficiency and safety of water treatment processes.</p></p>
