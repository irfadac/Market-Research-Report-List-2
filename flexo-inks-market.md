<p><strong>The Flexo Inks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent-based Printing Inks</li><li>Water-based Printing Inks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flexo inks market can be categorized into two main types: solvent-based printing inks and water-based printing inks. Solvent-based printing inks use organic solvents as the carrier for pigment and resin, offering fast drying times and excellent print quality on various substrates. On the other hand, water-based printing inks use water as the carrier, making them more eco-friendly and safer to use, with lower VOC emissions. Both types have their own advantages and are commonly used in the packaging industry for flexographic printing applications.</p></p>
