<p><ul><li>Bakeable</li><li>No Bakeable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fillings market can be categorized into two types: bakeable and no bakeable. Bakeable fillings are designed to be added to a dish before it is baked, allowing them to be cooked along with the rest of the ingredients. This type of filling is often used in dishes such as pies, quiches, and pastries. On the other hand, no bakeable fillings are not designed to be cooked, and are typically added to dishes after they have already been cooked or assembled. These fillings are often used in dishes such as cakes, sandwiches, and salads.</p></p>
