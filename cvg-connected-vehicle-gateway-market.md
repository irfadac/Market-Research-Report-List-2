<p><strong>The CVG (Connected Vehicle Gateway) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Central Gateway</li><li>LAN Gateway</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CVG market encompasses two main types: Central Gateway and LAN Gateway. Central Gateway acts as the main control unit for all connected vehicles within a network, managing communication between the vehicle and external systems. On the other hand, LAN Gateway focuses on local communication within the vehicle itself, enabling different systems and components to interact seamlessly. Both types of gateways play a crucial role in ensuring smooth and efficient communication within the connected vehicle ecosystem.</p></p>
