<p><p>ESD-Safe bags are designed to protect electronic components from electrostatic discharge. The market includes different types of bags such as antistatic bags, dissipative bags, and metallized bags. Antistatic bags prevent the buildup of static electricity on the surface, dissipative bags control the discharge of static electricity, and metallized bags provide a barrier to electromagnetic interference. Each type of bag offers varying degrees of protection to ensure the safe handling and storage of sensitive electronic components.</p></p>
