<p><p>Soybean protein market includes Soybean Protein Isolates, Soybean Protein Concentrates, and Soybean Protein Hydrolysates. Soybean Protein Isolates are highly refined proteins with minimum fats and carbohydrates. Soybean Protein Concentrates are less processed than isolates and contain a higher percentage of protein than the whole soybean. Soybean Protein Hydrolysates are broken down into smaller protein molecules, making them easier for the body to digest and absorb. Each type serves a specific nutritional purpose in various food and beverage applications.</p></p>
