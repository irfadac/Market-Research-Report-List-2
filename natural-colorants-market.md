<p><strong>The Natural Colorants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Obtained from Plants</li><li>Obtained from Animals</li><li>Obtained from Minerals</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Natural colorants can be categorized into three main types based on their sources: those obtained from plants, animals, and minerals. Plant-based colorants are derived from various parts of plants such as roots, leaves, and flowers. Animal-based colorants are sourced from animals like insects, shellfish, and beetles. Mineral-based colorants are extracted from natural minerals like iron oxide and titanium dioxide. Each type of natural colorant offers unique and vibrant shades that are increasingly in demand in various industries including food, cosmetics, and textiles.</p></p>
