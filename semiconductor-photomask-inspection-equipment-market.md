<p><strong>The Semiconductor Photomask Inspection Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Die to Die (DD) Method</li><li>Die to Database (DB) Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Semiconductor photomask inspection equipment market types include the Die to Die (DD) Method and Die to Database (DB) Method. The DD Method compares two identical dies on a photomask, focusing on defects or differences between them. On the other hand, the DB Method compares the die pattern against a reference database to identify any discrepancies. Both methods are essential for ensuring the quality and accuracy of photomasks used in semiconductor manufacturing processes.</p></p>
