<p><p>Casein and caseinates are protein derivatives commonly used in food and industrial applications. Sodium caseinate is used as a protein source and emulsifier in food products. Calcium caseinate is used in sports nutrition products for its slow-release protein content. Potassium caseinate is used in pharmaceuticals for its binding and disintegrating properties.</p></p>
