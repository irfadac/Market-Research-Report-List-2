<p><strong>The Automotive Clutch Cover Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coil Spring Clutch Cover</li><li>Diaphragm Spring Clutch Cover</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive clutch cover market is segmented into two main types - coil spring clutch cover and diaphragm spring clutch cover. Coil spring clutch covers use multiple coil springs to engage and disengage the clutch, providing a smoother operation and longer lifespan. Diaphragm spring clutch covers use a single diaphragm spring for the same purpose, offering easier maintenance and higher torque capacity. Both types serve the same function of transferring power from the engine to the transmission, with slight differences in design and performance.</p></p>
