<p><strong>The Xylylene Diisocyanate (XDI) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>XDI Monomer</li><li>XDI Prepolymer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Xylylene Diisocyanate (XDI) is used in two different market types - XDI Monomer and XDI Prepolymer. </p><p>XDI Monomer is a raw material used in the production of various polyurethane products, such as coatings, adhesives, sealants, and elastomers. It provides excellent durability and performance properties to these end products.</p><p>On the other hand, XDI Prepolymer is a partially reacted form of XDI, which is commonly used in applications where improved processing and handling properties are required. This allows for easier handling and processing during the manufacturing of polyurethane products.</p></p>
