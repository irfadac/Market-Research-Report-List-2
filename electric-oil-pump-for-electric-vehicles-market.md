<p><ul><li>Integrated Electric Oil Pump</li><li>Separate Electric Oil Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Integrated electric oil pumps are built into the electric vehicle's drivetrain system, providing lubrication and cooling for the motor and other components. They are more compact and efficient, reducing energy consumption and improving overall performance. Separate electric oil pumps are standalone units that can be mounted in various locations within the vehicle, providing more flexibility in design and installation. They are typically used in vehicles where space is limited or where a centralized lubrication system is not feasible.</p></p>
