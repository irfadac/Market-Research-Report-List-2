<p><p>Clear Fused Quartz is a type of quartz that has excellent optical clarity and is transparent. It is commonly used in applications where high visibility and precision are required, such as in lenses and prisms. Opaque Fused Quartz, on the other hand, is a type of quartz that does not allow light to pass through. It is often used in applications where thermal insulation or blocking of UV radiation is needed, such as in furnace tubes and semiconductor manufacturing. Both types of quartz are fabricated to meet specific needs in various industries.</p></p>
