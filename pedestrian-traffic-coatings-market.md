<p><p>Pedestrian traffic coatings are commonly categorized into one-component polyurethane and two-component polyurethane markets. One-component polyurethane coatings are pre-mixed and ready to use, offering a simple and convenient application process. On the other hand, two-component polyurethane coatings require mixing of two separate components before application, providing a more customizable and durable solution. Both types of coatings offer excellent durability, abrasion resistance, and waterproofing properties, making them ideal for protecting pedestrian walkways, parking decks, and other high-traffic areas.</p></p>
