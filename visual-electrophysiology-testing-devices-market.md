<p><strong>The Visual Electrophysiology Testing Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Multifocal Electroretinogram</li><li>Visual-Evoked Responses</li><li>Electroretinogram</li><li>Electro-Oculogram</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Visual electrophysiology testing devices market is segmented into different types based on the various tests they can perform. Multifocal electroretinogram devices measure specific regions of the retina's response to light stimuli, while visual-evoked response devices assess the transmission of visual information along the optic nerve. Electroretinogram devices evaluate the electrical activity of retinal cells, and electro-oculogram devices measure the electrical potential of the eye's retina and retinal pigmented epithelium. These devices play a crucial role in diagnosing and monitoring various eye conditions.</p></p>
