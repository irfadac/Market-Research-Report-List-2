<p><strong>The Household Water Purification System Market Industry Research by Application is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>POU</li><li>POE</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Household Water Purification System Market focuses on Point-of-Use (POU) systems and Point-of-Entry (POE) systems. POU systems are installed at the point where water is used, such as under the sink or on countertops, providing clean drinking water directly to the tap. POE systems treat water as it enters the household, ensuring that all water used throughout the home is purified. Both POU and POE systems are designed to remove contaminants and improve the overall quality of water for household use.</p></p>
