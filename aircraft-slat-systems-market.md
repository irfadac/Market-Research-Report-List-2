<p><p>Aircraft slats are movable panels located on the leading edge of an aircraft wing that extend during takeoff and landing to improve lift and reduce stall speed. Fixed leading edge systems are stationary and do not move. The market for slats generally includes various types such as single slotted, double slotted, and triple slotted slats. Fixed leading edge systems are typically found on aircraft with simpler wing designs that do not require movable slats for increased aerodynamic performance.</p></p>
