<p><ul><li>Wet Sea Salt</li><li>Dry Sea Salt</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Low Sodium Sea Salt is available in two main market types: wet sea salt and dry sea salt. </p><p>Wet sea salt is harvested by evaporating seawater to form salt crystals that retain moisture, giving it a slightly coarse texture and briny flavor. It is commonly used in cooking and as a finishing salt.</p><p>Dry sea salt, on the other hand, is harvested by evaporating seawater until only salt crystals remain. It has a finer texture and is often used in baking and preserving food. Both types offer health-conscious consumers a low-sodium alternative to traditional table salt.</p></p>
