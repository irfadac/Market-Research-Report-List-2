Sintered Fly Ash consists of fine particles of fly ash sintered at high temperatures. Stone Sludge is a byproduct of stone cutting and shaping processes. Expanded Perlite is created from volcanic glass. The "Others" category includes various additional types of lightweight aggregates.</p></p>
