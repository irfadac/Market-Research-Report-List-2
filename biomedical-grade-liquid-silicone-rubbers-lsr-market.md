<p><p>BioMedical Grade Liquid Silicone Rubbers (LSR) are commonly classified into two types: One-component Liquid Rubber and Two-component Liquid Rubber. One-component Liquid Rubber is ready to use and cures at room temperature, making it ideal for quick and easy applications. On the other hand, Two-component Liquid Rubber consists of base and curing agents that need to be mixed before application. This type allows for more precise control over curing time and properties, making it suitable for more complex projects in the biomedical industry.</p></p>
