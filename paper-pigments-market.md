<p><p>Paper pigments market can be categorized into three main types: calcium carbonate, kaolin, and others. Calcium carbonate pigments are widely used in the paper industry due to their brightness and opacity. Kaolin pigments are favored for their control over gloss and printability. Other pigments like talc and titanium dioxide also play a significant role in enhancing the physical and optical properties of paper. Each type of pigment offers unique characteristics that cater to different requirements in the paper manufacturing process.</p></p>
