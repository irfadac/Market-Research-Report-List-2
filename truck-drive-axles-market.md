<p><p>Truck drive axles come in three main market types: Single, Tandem, and Tridem. Single axles consist of one set of wheels and are commonly used in smaller vehicles. Tandem axles have two sets of wheels and provide better stability and load capacity, making them ideal for medium-sized trucks. Tridem axles feature three sets of wheels and are able to support even heavier loads, making them suitable for large trucks and commercial vehicles. Each type of drive axle is designed to meet different transportation needs and requirements.</p></p>
