<p><strong>The Residential RCCBs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>2 Poles</li><li>4 Poles</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Residential RCCBs are available in two main types: 2 poles and 4 poles. 2 pole RCCBs are commonly used in single-phase residential electrical systems, offering protection against earth faults. On the other hand, 4 pole RCCBs are typically used in three-phase residential systems, providing additional protection against neutral faults. Both types of RCCBs are essential for ensuring the safety of electrical systems in residential buildings by disconnecting the circuit in case of a fault to prevent electric shocks or fires.</p></p>
