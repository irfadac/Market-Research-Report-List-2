<p><p>Pet health insurance services come in two main types: comprehensive insurance and accident-only insurance. Comprehensive insurance covers a wide range of medical expenses for pets, including accidents, illnesses, and routine care. This type of coverage provides more comprehensive protection for pet owners, but typically comes with a higher premium. On the other hand, accident-only insurance specifically covers medical expenses resulting from accidents, such as broken bones or injuries from car accidents. This type of coverage is more limited but may be more affordable for pet owners looking for basic protection.</p></p>
