<p><strong>The Continuous Barge Unloader (CBU) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bridge Type</li><li>Cantilever Type</li><li>Elevating Type</li><li>Traveling Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Continuous Barge Unloaders (CBUs) come in various market types such as Bridge Type, Cantilever Type, Elevating Type, and Traveling Type. Bridge Type CBUs have a fixed bridge structure for unloading barges, while Cantilever Type CBUs have a horizontal arm that extends over the barge. Elevating Type CBUs have a vertical lift mechanism for unloading, and Traveling Type CBUs are mobile and can move along the length of the barge for efficient unloading. Each type caters to different barge unloading requirements in the market.</p></p>
