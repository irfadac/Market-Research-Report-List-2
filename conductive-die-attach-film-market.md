<p><ul><li>Electro-conductive Film</li><li>Non electro-conductive Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Conductive Die Attach Film market includes two main types - Electro-conductive Film and Non-electro-conductive Film. Electro-conductive films are designed to provide electrical conductivity between the die and the substrate, ensuring a reliable connection. These films are commonly used in electronic packaging applications. On the other hand, non-electro-conductive films are used when electrical conductivity is not required and are preferred for their thermal and mechanical properties. Both types play a crucial role in the semiconductor industry for bonding semiconductor chips to substrates during the manufacturing process.</p></p>
