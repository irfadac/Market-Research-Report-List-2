<p><p>Fishing floats come in two main market types: Ordinary fishing floats and electronic fishing floats. Ordinary fishing floats are traditional, non-electronic devices used to indicate fish bites by bobbing on the water's surface. Electronic fishing floats, on the other hand, are equipped with sensors or alarms that alert anglers to fish activity underwater. Both types of floats serve the same purpose of helping fishermen detect when a fish is biting, but electronic floats offer advanced technology for enhanced detection capabilities.</p></p>
