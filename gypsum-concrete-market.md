<p><p>Gypsum concrete is available in different forms such as powder, premix, and paste, catering to various construction needs. Powder gypsum concrete is in a dry form and requires mixing with water on-site. Premix gypsum concrete comes pre-mixed and is ready for installation after adding water. Paste gypsum concrete is a thick, ready-to-use mixture ideal for filling gaps and cracks. Each type offers different application methods and benefits, making them suitable for various construction projects.</p></p>
