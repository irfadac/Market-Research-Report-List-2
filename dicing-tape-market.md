<p><p>Dicing Tape Market Types can be categorized into UV Curable Type and Non-UV Type. UV Curable Type dicing tapes are cured by UV light, offering faster curing times and higher adhesion strength. Non-UV Type dicing tapes do not require UV light for curing and are suitable for applications where UV light exposure is not possible or desired. Both types of dicing tapes are commonly used in semiconductor manufacturing for wafer dicing processes.</p></p>
