<p><p>Structural Heart Imaging (SHI) is a diagnostic process that helps visualize and evaluate the structures of the heart in order to diagnose and treat various heart conditions. There are two main types of SHI market - Echocardiogram and Angiogram. Echocardiogram uses sound waves to create images of the heart, while Angiogram uses contrast dye and X-rays to visualize blood vessels and the flow of blood to the heart. Both types of imaging play a crucial role in providing valuable information for healthcare professionals to make accurate diagnoses and treatment plans for patients with heart-related issues.</p></p>
