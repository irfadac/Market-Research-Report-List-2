<p><p>Electrically operated tricycles come in two main market types: side wheel motors and center motors. Side wheel motors are positioned on either side of the rear wheels, providing stability and balance for the rider. On the other hand, center motors are located in the middle of the tricycle, offering better weight distribution and enhanced control. Both types cater to different preferences and needs of riders, whether it be increased stability or agility.</p></p>
