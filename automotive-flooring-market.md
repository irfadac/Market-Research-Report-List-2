<p><p>Automotive flooring can come in two main types: rubber material and woven material. Rubber material flooring is durable, easy to clean, and provides good traction for vehicle occupants. It is commonly used in all-weather floor mats and carpets. Woven material flooring, on the other hand, is often made of a combination of fabrics such as nylon, polyester, or vinyl. It offers a more stylish and luxurious look to the interior of a vehicle, making it popular in higher-end car models.</p></p>
