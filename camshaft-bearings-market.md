<p><p>Camshaft bearings can be categorized into two main types: Single Over-Head Cam (SOHC) Bearings and Dual Over-Head Cam (DOHC) Bearings. SOHC bearings are designed for engines with a single camshaft situated above the cylinder head, while DOHC bearings are used in engines with two camshafts located above the cylinder head. Both types play a critical role in supporting the camshafts and ensuring precise engine operation, making them essential components in the automotive industry.</p></p>
