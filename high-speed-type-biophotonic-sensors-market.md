<p><p>High Speed Type Biophotonic Sensors are categorized into two types: Two-Dimensional Sensor and Three-Dimensional Sensor. Two-Dimensional Sensors capture information on a flat surface, providing high-resolution images in real-time. They are commonly used in medical imaging and biological research. Three-Dimensional Sensors, on the other hand, capture depth information in addition to surface details, enabling more accurate and detailed imaging. They are used in applications such as biometrics, robotics, and virtual/augmented reality. Both types play a crucial role in advancing biophotonic technology.</p></p>
