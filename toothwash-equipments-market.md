<p><p>Toothwash equipments market includes two main types: Ultrasonic Scaling and Manual Scaling. Ultrasonic Scaling devices use high-frequency vibrations to remove plaque and tartar from teeth, making the cleaning process quicker and more efficient. On the other hand, Manual Scaling involves using handheld instruments to physically scrape away plaque and tartar. Both methods are commonly used by dentists and hygienists to clean teeth and maintain oral health. The choice between the two methods often depends on the patient's needs and preferences.</p></p>
