<p><p>Hollow microspheres are classified into two main types: dry hollow microspheres and wet hollow microspheres. Dry hollow microspheres are solid particles with a hollow center, often used in industries such as construction and automotive for their lightweight properties. Wet hollow microspheres, on the other hand, are filled with a liquid or gas, providing additional functionalities such as insulation or buoyancy. Both types of hollow microspheres offer unique benefits and applications across various industries.</p></p>
