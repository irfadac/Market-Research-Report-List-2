<p><strong>The Portable Spectrum Analyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Frequency Sweep Type Spectrum Analyzer</li><li>Real-Time Spectrum Analyzer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Frequency Sweep Type Spectrum Analyzer: This type of spectrum analyzer sweeps through a range of frequencies to analyze the signal strength at each frequency point. It is commonly used for measuring and analyzing frequency response characteristics of devices like amplifiers, filters, and antennas.</p><p>Real-Time Spectrum Analyzer: This type of spectrum analyzer processes and displays signals in real-time, providing instantaneous feedback on signal performance. It is beneficial for identifying intermittent or transient signals that may be missed by a frequency sweep type analyzer. Real-time spectrum analyzers are often used in applications requiring quick and accurate signal analysis, such as wireless communication testing and signal monitoring.</p></p>
