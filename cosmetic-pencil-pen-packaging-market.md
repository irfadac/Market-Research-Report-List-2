<p><p>Cosmetic pencil and pen packaging market consists of three main types: sharpenable wooden pencil, sharpenable molded pencil, and mechanical pencil. Sharpenable wooden pencils are made from wood and require manual sharpening. Sharpenable molded pencils are made from a moldable material such as plastic or clay and also require manual sharpening. Mechanical pencils have a refillable lead mechanism and do not require sharpening. Each type of packaging caters to different consumer preferences and usage needs in the cosmetic industry.</p></p>
