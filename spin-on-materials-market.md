<p><p>Spin-on materials market includes various types such as Spin-on Carbon Hardmasks (SOC) and Spin-on Metal Oxide Hardmasks (MHM) which are commonly used for advanced lithography processes in semiconductor industry. SOC provides high etch selectivity and pattern transfer performance, while MHM offers excellent thermal stability and good etch resistance. Other types in the market include spin-on dielectric materials, anti-reflective coatings, and planarization materials that play crucial roles in semiconductor fabrication processes. These materials are designed to meet specific requirements for advanced technology nodes in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
