<p><p>The automotive textiles market consists of three main types: woven, nonwoven, and composites. Woven textiles are created by weaving together yarns to form a fabric, offering strength and durability. Nonwoven textiles are made by bonding fibers together through mechanical, chemical, or thermal processes, providing versatility and cost-effectiveness. Composites combine different materials, such as textiles and plastics, to create lightweight and high-performance products. Each type of automotive textile offers unique characteristics to meet the diverse needs of the automotive industry.</p></p>
