<p><p>Piezoelectric transducers market types include disks, plates, tubes, and others. Disks are circular in shape and are commonly used for generating ultrasonic sound waves or vibrations. Plates are flat, rectangular-shaped transducers that are effective for sensing and generating vibrations. Tubes are cylindrical in shape and are used for applications requiring a higher sensitivity to pressure changes. Other types of piezoelectric transducers may include sensors, actuators, and resonators, each designed for specific functions within various industries.</p></p>
