<p><p>Airborne LiDAR systems are categorized into two main types: Topographic LiDAR and Bathymetric LiDAR. Topographic LiDAR is used for mapping land surfaces, vegetation, buildings, and other above-ground features. It is widely used in applications such as urban planning, forestry management, and infrastructure development. On the other hand, Bathymetric LiDAR is specifically designed for mapping underwater terrain, such as seafloors and riverbeds. This type of LiDAR is commonly employed in marine surveys, coastal zone management, and underwater exploration.</p></p>
