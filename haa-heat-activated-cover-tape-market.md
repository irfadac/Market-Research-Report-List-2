<p><strong>The HAA (Heat Activated) Cover Tape Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-conductive</li><li>Static Dissipative</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Non-conductive heat-activated cover tape is designed to prevent the flow of electricity, making it ideal for protecting components that are sensitive to static electricity. This type of cover tape is commonly used in electronics manufacturing to secure and protect sensitive components during transportation and storage. </p><p>On the other hand, static dissipative heat-activated cover tape is formulated to safely dissipate static electricity, reducing the risk of damage to sensitive electronic components. This type of cover tape is essential in applications where static electricity can cause malfunction or damage to equipment.</p></p>
