<p><p>Automotive air flow sensors measure the amount of air entering an engine to ensure optimal performance. There are two main types of air flow sensors - Vane Meter Type and Hot Wire Type. Vane Meter Type sensors use a movable flap to measure air flow, while Hot Wire Type sensors use a heated wire to measure changes in air flow. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining fuel efficiency, emissions control, and overall engine performance in vehicles.</p></p>
