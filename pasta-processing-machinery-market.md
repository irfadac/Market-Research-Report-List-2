<p><p>Pasta processing machinery can be classified into two main types: fully automatic and semi-automatic. Fully automatic machinery operates with minimal human intervention, performing all processing tasks automatically from mixing to packaging. On the other hand, semi-automatic machinery requires some level of manual operation, such as loading ingredients or controlling certain processes. Both types of machines cater to the needs of different manufacturers, depending on their production scale, budget, and level of automation desired.</p></p>
