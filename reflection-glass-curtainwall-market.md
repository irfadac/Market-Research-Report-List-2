<p><p>Reflection Glass Curtainwall Market Types include Single Glazed Type, Double Glazed Type, and Three Glazed Type. Single Glazed Type curtainwall consists of a single glass pane, offering basic insulation and limited sound protection. Double Glazed Type features two glass panes with an air gap in between for improved insulation and sound reduction. Three Glazed Type includes three glass panes with two air gaps, providing the highest level of insulation and soundproofing. These different types cater to various needs and preferences in the construction industry.</p></p>
