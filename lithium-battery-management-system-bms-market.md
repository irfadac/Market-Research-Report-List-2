<p><p>Lithium Battery Management System (BMS) market is divided into two main types: Centralized BMS and Distributed BMS. Centralized BMS involves a single control unit that manages the whole battery pack, making it suitable for simpler applications. Distributed BMS, on the other hand, consists of multiple independent control units, offering more flexibility and scalability for complex battery systems. Both types have their own advantages and are used in various applications depending on the specific requirements of the system.</p></p>
