<p><p>Micro UAVs are categorized into three main market types: Rotary Airfoil, Fixed-Wing, and Others. Rotary Airfoil UAVs utilize rotating blades to generate lift and propulsion, allowing for vertical take-off and landing capabilities. Fixed-Wing UAVs, on the other hand, have a fixed wing structure that enables efficient forward flight. The Others category includes hybrid designs or unconventional UAV configurations that do not fit neatly into the previous two categories. Each market type offers unique advantages and is tailored to specific applications and operational requirements.</p></p>
