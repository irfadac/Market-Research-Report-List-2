<p><p>Engineering plastics are categorized into two main types: general engineering plastics and special engineering plastics. General engineering plastics are commonly used in a wide range of applications due to their versatile properties, such as high strength, heat resistance, and chemical resistance. On the other hand, special engineering plastics are designed for specific applications that require superior performance in extreme conditions, such as high temperatures, corrosive environments, or electrical insulation. These specialized plastics offer unique properties tailored to meet the demanding requirements of specific industries.</p></p>
