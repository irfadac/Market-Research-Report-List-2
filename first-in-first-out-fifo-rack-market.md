<p><strong>The First In First Out (FIFO) Rack Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Selective Pallet Rack</li><li>Gravity Flow Rack</li><li>Pass-through Rack</li></ul></p>
<p><p>FIFO rack market types include Selective Pallet Rack, Gravity Flow Rack, and Pass-through Rack. Selective Pallet Rack allows for easy access to every pallet, making it ideal for high turnover inventory. Gravity Flow Rack uses inclined rollers to allow pallets to flow smoothly, ensuring the first pallet in is the first pallet out. Pass-through Rack design allows for loading on one side and unloading on the other, promoting FIFO inventory management. Each type caters to different storage needs while prioritizing the FIFO method.</p></p>
