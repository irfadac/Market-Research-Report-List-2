<p><p>Advanced High-Strength Steels (AHSS) are categorized into three main types based on their alloy content - Low Alloy, Medium Alloy, and High Alloy. Low Alloy AHSS have a lower alloy content and are commonly used in automotive applications. Medium Alloy AHSS have a moderate alloy content and offer a balance of strength and formability. High Alloy AHSS have a higher alloy content, providing superior strength and resistance to corrosion. These different types of AHSS cater to various industry needs and requirements.</p></p>
