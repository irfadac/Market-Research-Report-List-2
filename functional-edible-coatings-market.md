<p><p>Functional edible coatings market can be classified into various types based on their composition. Collagen-based coatings are derived from natural proteins and are known for their ability to improve food texture and appearance. Protein-based coatings are commonly used to enhance food shelf life and protect against moisture loss. Polysaccharides are carbohydrates that form a protective barrier on the food surface, preventing oxidation and microbial growth. Other types of edible coatings may include lipid-based coatings or composite coatings made from a combination of different materials.</p></p>
