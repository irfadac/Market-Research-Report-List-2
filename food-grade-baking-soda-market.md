<p><p>Food Grade Baking Soda is manufactured through three main methods in the market: Sodium Carbonate Method, Trona Method, and Ammonium Carbonate Method. The Sodium Carbonate Method involves the reaction of sodium carbonate with carbon dioxide to produce baking soda. The Trona Method uses trona ore as a source of sodium carbonate which reacts with carbon dioxide. The Ammonium Carbonate Method combines ammonia, carbon dioxide, and water to form baking soda. Each method produces high-quality food grade baking soda for various commercial and industrial applications.</p></p>
