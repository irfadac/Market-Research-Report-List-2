<p><p>PE Synthetic Turf and PP Synthetic Turf are two types of eco-friendly turf markets. PE Synthetic Turf is made from polyethylene, offering a softer feel and more natural look. It is commonly used for sports fields and landscaping. On the other hand, PP Synthetic Turf is made from polypropylene and is more affordable. It is suitable for low-traffic areas like residential lawns. Both types of turf are sustainable alternatives to natural grass, requiring minimal water, pesticides, and maintenance.</p></p>
