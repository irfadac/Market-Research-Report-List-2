<p><p>Pigment additives market is divided into two main types - inorganic pigments and organic pigments. Inorganic pigments are made from mineral sources and are known for their durability and resistance to heat and light. They are commonly used in industries such as construction, automotive, and ceramics. On the other hand, organic pigments are derived from carbon-based compounds and offer a wide range of colors and shades. They are widely used in industries such as printing, textiles, and plastics.</p></p>
