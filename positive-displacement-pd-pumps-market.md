<p><p>Positive Displacement (PD) pumps are divided into three main types: Reciprocating, Rotary, and Others. Reciprocating pumps use a piston or diaphragm to displace the fluid, providing a pulsating flow. Rotary pumps use rotating elements, such as gears or lobes, to move the fluid continuously. Other types of PD pumps include screw pumps and peristaltic pumps. Each type of pump has unique advantages and applications, catering to different industries and fluid handling requirements.</p></p>
