There are two main market types of UHETs: single-flush type and dual-flush type. Single-flush UHETs have a consistent flush volume for all solid and liquid waste, while dual-flush UHETs offer two flush options - a lower volume for liquid waste and a higher volume for solid waste. Both types of UHETs are designed to conserve water and reduce water usage.</p></p>
