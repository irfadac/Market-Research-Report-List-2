<p><p>Automatic Tapping Machines are classified into two main types: Fully automatic Tapping Machines and Semi-automatic Tapping Machines. Fully automatic Tapping Machines are designed to perform tapping operations without any manual intervention, providing high efficiency and productivity. On the other hand, Semi-automatic Tapping Machines require manual loading and unloading of parts but automate the tapping process itself, offering a balance between automation and flexibility. Both market segments cater to different needs and requirements of industries looking for tapping solutions.</p></p>
