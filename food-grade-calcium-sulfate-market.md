<p><p>Food Grade Calcium Sulfate is commonly available in two main forms in the market: powder and lump. Powdered calcium sulfate is finely ground and easy to dissolve in liquid, making it ideal for use in food and beverage applications where a smooth texture is desired. Lump calcium sulfate, on the other hand, consists of larger, solid pieces that are often used in industrial settings for their slower release of calcium ions. Both forms are valued for their ability to fortify products with calcium while meeting safety standards for food grade materials.</p></p>
