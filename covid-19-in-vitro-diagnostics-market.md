<p><p>COVID-19 In-Vitro Diagnostics market includes Rapid Diagnostic Test (RDT), Enzyme Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA), and Neutralization Assay types. RDTs provide quick results by detecting specific antigens in patient samples. ELISA tests measure antibodies or antigens in blood samples to identify if a person has been exposed to the virus. Neutralization Assays determine the ability of antibodies to neutralize viruses. These different types of tests play a crucial role in diagnosing and monitoring COVID-19 infections.</p></p>
