<p><p>Electro-Pneumatic Train Brakes have various types that are commonly used in the market. Self-Lapping Brakes are designed to automatically adjust brake pressure to maintain a desired level. Retardation Controllers are used to regulate braking force to achieve smooth deceleration. P-Wire Control systems utilize electronic controls to manage brake functions. Variable Load Control systems adjust braking power based on the weight of the train. Each type offers unique features and benefits to improve the safety and efficiency of train braking systems.</p></p>
