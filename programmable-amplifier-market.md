<p><ul><li>Resistive</li><li>Capacitive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Programmable amplifiers come in various types, such as resistive and capacitive market. Resistive programmable amplifiers use resistors to control the gain and impedance of the amplifier circuit, allowing for precise adjustment of the amplifier's characteristics. On the other hand, capacitive programmable amplifiers use capacitors to control the frequency response and bandwidth of the amplifier. Both types offer flexibility in tuning the amplifier's performance to meet specific requirements in various applications, making them popular choices in the market for customizable amplifier solutions.</p></p>
