<p><p>Intraoral Dental X-ray Systems market includes different types of X-rays such as Bitewing X-rays, Periapical X-rays, and Occlusal X-rays. Bitewing X-rays focus on the upper and lower teeth to detect cavities and bone level. Periapical X-rays show the entire tooth and surrounding bone to identify dental issues like infections or abscesses. Occlusal X-rays capture the entire arch of teeth in one image for assessing tooth development and detecting issues like impacted teeth. These different types cater to specific dental diagnostic needs.</p></p>
