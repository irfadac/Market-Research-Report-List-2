<p><p>Cordless drill driver market is segmented into two types - pneumatic and electric. Pneumatic drill drivers are powered by compressed air, making them lightweight and easy to use, but requiring an air compressor for operation. Electric drill drivers, on the other hand, are powered by rechargeable batteries, offering more flexibility and portability. Both types cater to different user needs and preferences, with pneumatic drills being popular in industrial settings and electric drills being preferred for household tasks.</p></p>
