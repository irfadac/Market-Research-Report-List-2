<p><p>Synthetic Polymer Waterproofing Membrane market includes Sheet Based Waterproofing Membrane and Liquid Applied Membranes. Sheet Based Waterproofing Membrane is a preformed waterproofing material that comes in large sheets and is applied by rolling or adhering to a surface. Liquid Applied Membranes, on the other hand, are applied as a liquid coating that cures into a seamless, continuous membrane. Both types of membranes are used to protect structures from water damage and provide long-lasting waterproofing solutions.</p></p>
