<p><p>Box awnings are available in two main market types: manual and motorized. Manual box awnings are operated by hand, typically using a crank or handle to extend and retract the awning. These are ideal for smaller awnings and areas where electricity accessibility is limited. Motorized box awnings, on the other hand, are equipped with a motor that allows for easy and convenient operation with just the push of a button. These are popular for larger awnings or for those seeking added convenience and ease of use.</p></p>
