<p><p>Bitumen Tape Market consists of two main types- Double Sided Bitumen Tape and Single Sided Bitumen Tape. Double Sided Bitumen Tape is designed for applications where both sides need to adhere to different surfaces, providing a strong and long-lasting bond. On the other hand, Single Sided Bitumen Tape is suitable for applications where only one side needs to be adhered to a surface, offering protection against water and moisture intrusion. Both types of bitumen tapes are widely used in construction, waterproofing, and roofing industries.</p></p>
