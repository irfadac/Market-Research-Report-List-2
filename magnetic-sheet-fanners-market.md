<p><p>Magnetic sheet fanners are devices used to separate stacked metal sheets by creating a magnetic field. There are two main types of magnetic sheet fanners: permanent magnet and electromagnetic. Permanent magnet fanners use fixed magnets to create a magnetic field, while electromagnetic fanners use an electric current to generate a magnetic field. Permanent magnet fanners are cost-effective and require minimal maintenance, while electromagnetic fanners offer greater flexibility and control over the magnetic field strength. Both types provide efficient sheet separation in industrial applications.</p></p>
