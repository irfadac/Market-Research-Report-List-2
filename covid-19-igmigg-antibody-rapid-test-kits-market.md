<p><p>The COVID-19 IgM/IgG Antibody Rapid Test Kits Market includes three main types: RDT (Rapid Diagnostic Test), ELISA (Enzyme-linked Immunosorbent Assay), and Neutralization Assay. RDTs provide quick results by detecting antibodies in blood samples. ELISA tests are highly sensitive and specific, measuring the level of antibodies in a sample. Neutralization Assays determine the ability of antibodies to block the virus from infecting cells. Each type of test plays a crucial role in detecting and monitoring COVID-19 infections.</p></p>
