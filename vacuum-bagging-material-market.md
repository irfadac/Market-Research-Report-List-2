<p><ul><li>Vacuum Bagging Film</li><li>Release Film</li><li>Peel Ply</li><li>Breather/Bleeder</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vacuum bagging materials market includes various types such as vacuum bagging film, release film, peel ply, breather/bleeder, and others. Vacuum bagging film is used to create a vacuum-tight seal around the part being infused with resin. Release film is placed between the vacuum bagging film and part to prevent sticking. Peel ply is used to create a rough surface for bonding. Breather/bleeder materials allow excess resin and air to escape during the infusion process. Other materials may include sealant tape, vacuum connectors, and infusion mesh.</p></p>
