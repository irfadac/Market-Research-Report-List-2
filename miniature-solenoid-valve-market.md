<p><p>Miniature solenoid valves are categorized into three main types based on their configurations: 2-way, 3-way, and 4-way solenoid valves. </p><p>2-way solenoid valves have one inlet and one outlet and are commonly used for on/off applications. </p><p>3-way solenoid valves have three ports and are used to divert flow between two different paths or to control flow to a single output. </p><p>4-way solenoid valves have four ports and are used for more complex flow control applications. </p><p>There are also other types of miniature solenoid valves available in the market to cater to specific industrial requirements.</p></p>
