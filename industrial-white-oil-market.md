<p><strong>The Industrial White Oil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Paraffin-based</li><li>Cycloalkyl</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Paraffin-based industrial white oil is derived from straight-chain hydrocarbons and is known for its high purity and stability. It is commonly used in industries such as cosmetics, pharmaceuticals, and plastics. On the other hand, Cycloalkyl industrial white oil is derived from cyclic hydrocarbons and is valued for its excellent lubricating properties and thermal stability. It finds applications in industries like textiles, automotive, and food packaging. Both types of industrial white oil offer different properties and benefits, catering to a wide range of industrial needs.</p></p>
