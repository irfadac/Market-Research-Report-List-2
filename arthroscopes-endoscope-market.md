<p><p>Arthroscopes endoscope market types can be categorized into straight, semi-flexible, and bent endoscopes. Straight endoscopes are rigid and are used for procedures that require precise, straight access to the joint. Semi-flexible endoscopes offer some flexibility and can be maneuvered around corners or tight spaces within the joint. Bent endoscopes have a curved design that allows for better visualization of hard-to-reach areas within the joint. Each type of endoscope caters to different needs in orthopedic and arthroscopic procedures.</p></p>
