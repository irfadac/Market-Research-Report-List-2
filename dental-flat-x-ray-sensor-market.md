<p><p>Dental flat X-ray sensors are available in two market types - CCD Technology and CMOS Technology. CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) sensors offer high image quality with low noise levels, making them ideal for detailed dental imaging. On the other hand, the CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) sensors are more cost-effective and energy efficient, making them a popular choice for dental practices looking for a budget-friendly option. Both technologies have their own set of advantages and are widely used in the dental industry for capturing high-quality X-ray images.</p></p>
