<p><p>Covid-19 serology testing market includes various types of tests such as Rapid Diagnostic Test (RDT), Enzyme Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA), and Neutralization Assay. RDTs provide quick results by detecting antibodies in blood samples. ELISA tests measure levels of specific antibodies in the blood to determine past infection. Neutralization assays determine the ability of antibodies to neutralize the virus. Each type of test plays a crucial role in providing information about an individual's immune response to Covid-19.</p></p>
