<p><strong>The Creping Adhesives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cross-Linked Polymer Creping Adhesive</li><li>Non-Cross-Linked Polymer Creping Adhesives</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Creping adhesives are used in the paper manufacturing industry to control the release of paper from the creping cylinder. Cross-linked polymer creping adhesives offer higher strength and durability due to the chemical bonds formed between polymer chains. On the other hand, non-cross-linked polymer creping adhesives do not have these chemical bonds, making them more flexible and easier to remove during the paper manufacturing process. Each type of creping adhesive offers unique advantages and is chosen based on specific requirements in the paper production process.</p></p>
