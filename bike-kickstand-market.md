<p><p>The bike kickstand market can be categorized into two main types - the side kickstand and the two-leg double kickstand. Two-leg double kickstands, on the other hand, have two legs that extend out from both sides of the bike, offering a more secure and balanced support. Both types cater to different needs and preferences of cyclists, with side kickstands being more common and two-leg double kickstands providing added stability for heavier bikes.</p></p>
