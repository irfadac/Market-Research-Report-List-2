<p><p>Soft ferrite cores are categorized into three main types: Nickel-Zinc, Manganese-Zinc, and Magnesium-Zinc. Nickel-Zinc ferrite cores offer high permeability and low losses, making them suitable for high-frequency applications. Manganese-Zinc ferrite cores have excellent temperature stability and are commonly used in power applications. Magnesium-Zinc ferrite cores provide high magnetic saturation and are often used in inductors and transformers. Each type of soft ferrite core has specific characteristics that make them suitable for different electronic applications.</p></p>
