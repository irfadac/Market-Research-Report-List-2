<p><strong>The Aircraft Deicing Fluids Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li><li>Type IV</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aircraft deicing fluids market is divided into Type I, Type II, and Type IV fluids. Type I fluids are heated and provide rapid deicing at a high efficiency, ideal for light to moderate ice accumulation. Type II fluids are diluted with water and offer longer holdover times, making them suitable for heavier ice buildup. Type IV fluids are designed for anti-icing and provide extended protection against ice accumulation, making them suitable for longer flight durations in frigid conditions.</p></p>
