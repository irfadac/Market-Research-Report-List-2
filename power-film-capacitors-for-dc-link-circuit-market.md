<p><ul><li>Fixed Capacitors</li><li>Variable Capacitors</li><li>Trimmer Capacitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Power film capacitors for DC link circuits can be categorized into three main types - fixed capacitors, variable capacitors, and trimmer capacitors. Fixed capacitors have a set capacitance value that cannot be adjusted. Variable capacitors allow for the capacitance to be adjusted manually or electronically. Trimmer capacitors are specialized variable capacitors that are adjusted during the manufacturing process to fine-tune the capacitance value. Each type of capacitor serves a specific purpose in DC link circuits, providing flexibility and reliability in various applications.</p></p>
