<p><p>Optical telescopes are devices used to observe distant objects by collecting and focusing light. There are three main types of optical telescopes on the market. Refracting telescopes utilize lenses to refract light and produce magnified images. Reflecting telescopes use mirrors to reflect and focus light. Catadioptric telescopes combine both lenses and mirrors for enhanced image quality. Each type offers unique advantages and applications, catering to different needs and preferences of astronomers and enthusiasts.</p></p>
