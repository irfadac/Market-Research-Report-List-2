<p><p>Car Exhaust Heat Recovery Systems are aimed at harnessing waste heat from car exhaust gases and converting it into usable energy. The Exhaust Gas Heat Recovery (EGHR) system captures and transfers heat from the exhaust gas to a fluid, which can then be used to power various vehicle systems. Rankine Cycle Systems use a similar principle but operate on a closed-loop system to generate electricity. Other market types include thermoelectric generators, turbo-compounding systems, and organic Rankine cycle systems, all aiming to improve fuel efficiency and reduce emissions in vehicles.</p></p>
