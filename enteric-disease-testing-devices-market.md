<p><p>Enteric Disease Testing Devices Market includes different types of testing methods such as Immunoassay Testing, Conventional Testing, and Molecular Diagnostic Testing. Immunoassay Testing involves detecting specific antibodies or antigens in the stool. Conventional Testing includes methods like culture and microscopy to identify pathogens. Molecular Diagnostic Testing involves detecting the genetic material of pathogens in the stool. Each type of testing offers advantages and limitations in terms of sensitivity, specificity, and turnaround time, catering to different clinical needs.</p></p>
