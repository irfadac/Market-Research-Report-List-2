<p><p>The edible thickener market includes various types such as Animal Glue, Vegetable Gum, Seaweed Gum, Microbial Glue, Starch, and Others. Animal Glue is derived from animal collagen and is commonly used in food products. Vegetable Gum is sourced from plants and is a popular choice for food thickening. Seaweed Gum is extracted from seaweed and is known for its thickening properties. Microbial Glue is produced by microorganisms and used as a thickener in food manufacturing. Starch is a common thickening agent derived from plants. Others include various synthetic or natural substances used for thickening food products.</p></p>
