<p><p>Armored fiber patch cables are categorized into single-mode and multi-mode fiber patch cables based on the type of optical fibers used in the construction. Single-mode fiber patch cables are designed for long-distance communication with a single mode of light transmission, while multi-mode fiber patch cables are suitable for shorter distances with multiple modes of light transmission. Both types offer enhanced protection with armored jackets, making them ideal for harsh environments where cables may be prone to damage from elements such as moisture, rodents, or debris.</p></p>
