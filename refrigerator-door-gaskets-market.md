<p><p>Refrigerator door gaskets are available in two main types - rubber sleeve and magnetic strip. Rubber sleeve gaskets are made of flexible rubber material that fits snugly around the door to create an airtight seal. On the other hand, magnetic strip gaskets have a magnetic strip embedded within the rubber material to help ensure a tight seal when the door is closed. Both types of gaskets serve the same purpose of maintaining the temperature inside the refrigerator and preventing energy loss.</p></p>
