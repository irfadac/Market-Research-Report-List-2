<p><ul><li>Micro Minerals</li><li>Macro Minerals</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Feed minerals market can be classified into two types: Micro Minerals and Macro Minerals. Micro Minerals are essential minerals needed in small quantities by animals for various bodily functions like enzyme reactions and growth. Examples include iron, copper, and zinc. Macro Minerals, on the other hand, are required in larger amounts and play a crucial role in maintaining body functions like bone growth and muscle contraction. Examples of macro minerals include calcium, phosphorus, and magnesium. Both micro and macro minerals are vital for animal health and overall development.</p></p>
