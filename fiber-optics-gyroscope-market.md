<p><p>Fiber optics gyroscope market is categorized into three types based on the number of axes they measure: 1 Axis, 2 Axis, and 3 Axis. 1 Axis gyroscopes measure rotation around one axis, typically used in simpler applications. 2 Axis gyroscopes measure rotation around two axes, providing more accurate measurements and are commonly used in aerospace and marine navigation. 3 Axis gyroscopes measure rotation around all three axes, offering the most precise readings and are crucial in advanced applications like unmanned vehicles and missile guidance systems.</p></p>
