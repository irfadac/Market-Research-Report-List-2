<p><strong>The Automotive HUDs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Windshield Projected HUD</li><li>Combiner Projected HUD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive HUDs are classified into two main types: windshield projected HUDs and combiner projected HUDs. Windshield projected HUDs display information directly onto the windshield, providing the driver with key data such as speed, navigation directions, and warnings without having to take their eyes off the road. Combiner projected HUDs, on the other hand, use a small transparent screen mounted on the dashboard to reflect information onto the windshield. Both types of HUDs enhance driver safety by presenting crucial information in the driver's line of sight.</p></p>
