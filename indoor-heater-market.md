<p><strong>The Indoor Heater Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radiative Space Heater</li><li>Convective Space Heater</li><li>Filler Space Heater</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The indoor heater market can be categorized into different types based on their heating mechanism. Radiative space heaters use infrared radiation to directly heat objects and people in a room. Convective space heaters circulate air through heating elements to warm the space. Filler space heaters use a filler material like oil or water to store and release heat slowly. Other types of heaters in the market include ceramic space heaters, panel heaters, and fan heaters, each with their own unique way of heating indoor spaces.</p></p>
