<p><p>Cloud-based e-discovery software market consists of three main types: public cloud, private cloud, and hybrid cloud. Public cloud is where software and services are hosted on a third-party's server and accessed through the internet. Private cloud is a dedicated environment hosted on a company's own servers or through a private provider. Hybrid cloud combines elements of both public and private clouds, enabling businesses to customize their e-discovery solutions to meet specific needs while leveraging the benefits of both types of cloud environments.</p></p>
