<p><p>The Fluoro Compounds market can be classified into two main types: Organofluorine Compounds and Inorganic Fluorine Compounds. Organofluorine compounds are organic compounds containing one or more carbon-fluorine bonds, widely used in industries like pharmaceuticals, agrochemicals, and materials. Inorganic Fluorine Compounds, on the other hand, consist of fluorides of elements like sodium, potassium, and aluminum, primarily used in applications such as water fluoridation, metal manufacturing, and glass etching. Both types play crucial roles in various industries due to their unique characteristics and properties.</p></p>
