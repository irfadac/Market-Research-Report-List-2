<p><p>High barrier resin market is categorized into different types such as PEN, PVDC, EVOH, and others. PEN (Polyethylene Naphthalate) offers excellent barrier properties against gases and light, making it suitable for packaging applications. PVDC (Polyvinylidene Chloride) provides high oxygen and moisture barrier, commonly used in food packaging. EVOH (Ethylene Vinyl Alcohol) resins offer exceptional barrier properties against oxygen and other gases. Other high barrier resins include materials like nylon and fluoropolymers, each offering unique barrier properties for specific packaging needs.</p></p>
