<p><p>Darkroom safelights are designed to provide low levels of illumination in darkrooms without affecting light-sensitive materials. There are three main types of safelights in the market: interchangeable filter safelights, bare bulb safelights, and LED safelights. Interchangeable filter safelights allow users to change the color of the light emitted, while bare bulb safelights use a colored bulb to emit light. LED safelights use light emitting diodes to emit a specific color of light. Each type of safelight has its own advantages and is suitable for different darkroom environments.</p></p>
