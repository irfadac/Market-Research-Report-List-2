<p><p>Mask Writers for the semiconductor market include laser mask writers and electron beam mask writers. Laser mask writers use laser beams to pattern images on masks, providing high throughput at a lower cost. On the other hand, electron beam mask writers offer higher resolution and accuracy, suitable for producing masks with intricate patterns. Both types of mask writers play a critical role in the semiconductor industry by enabling the production of complex integrated circuits for electronic devices.</p></p>
