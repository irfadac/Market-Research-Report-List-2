<p><p>Bio-plastic paper market includes three main types - RPD paper, RBD paper, and others. RPD paper refers to recycled paper with disposable bio-plastic coating, providing both recyclability and biodegradability. RBD paper is made from virgin pulp with bio-plastic coating, offering a more sustainable alternative to traditional paper. The "Others" category encompasses various types of bio-plastic paper products that may not fall into the RPD or RBD categories, but still contribute to reducing environmental impact compared to conventional paper products.</p></p>
