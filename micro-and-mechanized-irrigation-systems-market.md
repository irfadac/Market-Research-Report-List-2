<p><p>Micro and mechanized irrigation systems market types include Sprinkler, Drip, Central Pivot, and Lateral Move systems. Sprinkler systems distribute water through overhead sprinklers, while Drip systems deliver water directly to the roots of plants. Central Pivot systems have a rotating sprinkler mounted on a central pivot point, covering a circular area. Lateral Move systems move along a straight line to irrigate a rectangular area. These systems help improve water efficiency, reduce labor costs, and increase crop yields in agricultural settings.</p></p>
