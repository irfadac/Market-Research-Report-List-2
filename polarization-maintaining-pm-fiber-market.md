<p><p>Polarization Maintaining (PM) Fiber market consists of two main types: Linear Polarization Maintaining Fiber (LPMF) and Circular Polarization Maintaining Fiber (CPMF). LPMF is designed to maintain the linear polarization state of light propagating through the fiber, while CPMF is engineered to maintain the circular polarization state. These specialized fibers are used in various applications where maintaining specific polarization states is crucial, such as in telecommunications, fiber optic sensors, and high-power fiber lasers.</p></p>
