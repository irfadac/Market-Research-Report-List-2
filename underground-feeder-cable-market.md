<p><ul><li>Solid</li><li>Twisted</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Solid Underground Feeder Cable is made up of a single solid conductor that is typically used for long runs in straight lines. This type of cable is known for its stability and reliability. On the other hand, Twisted Underground Feeder Cable consists of multiple twisted conductors that are used to reduce interference and crosstalk. This type of cable is commonly used in environments where there are high levels of electromagnetic interference. Both types of cables have their own unique characteristics and are chosen based on specific project requirements.</p></p>
