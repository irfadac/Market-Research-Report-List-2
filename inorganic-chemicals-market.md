<p><p>The inorganic chemicals market is divided into different types such as non-metallic oxides, inorganic salts, metal oxides, alkalis, and inorganic acids. Non-metallic oxides are compounds composed of oxygen and a non-metal element. Inorganic salts are compounds formed from the reaction of an acid with a base. Metal oxides are compounds containing a metal cation and oxygen anion. Alkalis are soluble bases that release hydroxide ions in water. Inorganic acids are compounds that release hydrogen ions in water.</p></p>
