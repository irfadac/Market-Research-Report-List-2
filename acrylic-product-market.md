<p><p>Acrylic products market is divided into two main types: Cast Acrylic Sheet and Extruded Acrylic Sheet. Cast acrylic sheets are made by pouring liquid acrylic into molds and allowing it to harden, resulting in a high-quality and optical clarity product suitable for a wide range of applications. On the other hand, extruded acrylic sheets are created by extruding acrylic through a machine, resulting in a more cost-effective option with slightly lower quality. Both types have their own advantages and are widely used in various industries.</p></p>
