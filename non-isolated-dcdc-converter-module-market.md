<p><p>Non-Isolated DC/DC Converter Modules are used to convert DC voltage levels and deliver reliable power in various electronic applications. Boost Modules are designed to increase output voltage levels while Buck Modules decrease voltage levels. Boost Modules are commonly used when the output voltage needs to be higher than the input voltage, such as in solar power systems. Buck Modules, on the other hand, are used to step down voltage levels, making them suitable for applications like battery charging. Both types offer efficient power conversion solutions for different requirements.</p></p>
