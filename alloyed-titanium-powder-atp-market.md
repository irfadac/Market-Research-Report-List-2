<p><p>Alloyed Titanium Powder (ATP) is available in different types based on the alloying elements used. Ti-101 is a type of ATP with low oxygen content, making it suitable for applications requiring high strength and corrosion resistance. Ti-201 contains aluminum and vanadium for improved mechanical properties, making it ideal for aerospace and automotive industries. Ti-301 is a beta-stabilized ATP with molybdenum and palladium additions, offering excellent strength and creep resistance for high-temperature applications. Each type of ATP caters to specific industry needs based on its alloy composition.</p></p>
