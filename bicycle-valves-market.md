<p><p>Bicycle valves are commonly divided into two main types: rubber tire valves and metal tire valves. Rubber tire valves are made of flexible rubber material that provides a seal between the valve and the tire. They are typically used for smaller bicycles and are cheaper compared to metal tire valves. On the other hand, metal tire valves are more durable and long-lasting, making them ideal for high-performance bicycles and heavy-duty applications. Both types of valves serve the same purpose of inflating and deflating bicycle tires but cater to different needs based on durability and price.</p></p>
