<p><p>Automotive System-on-Chips (SoCs) cater to various market types including Analog ICs, Microcontrollers, Logic ICs, Memory, ECU, and Others. Logic ICs are responsible for digital circuit functions, Memory stores data and programs, ECU manages the electronic systems in a vehicle. The Others category includes specialized chips for specific applications. These different types of chips work together to optimize performance and efficiency in automotive systems.</p></p>
