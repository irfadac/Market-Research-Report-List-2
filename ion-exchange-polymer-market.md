<p><p>Ion exchange polymers are widely used in various industries for purification purposes. Cationic exchange polymers selectively exchange positive ions, while anionic exchange polymers selectively exchange negative ions. Other types of exchange polymers include amphoteric polymers, which can exchange both positive and negative ions. Each type of exchange polymer serves a unique purpose based on the desired ion exchange properties. These polymers play a crucial role in water treatment, chemical processing, and pharmaceutical industries by effectively removing impurities from solutions.</p></p>
