<p><p>Industrial displacement sensors are used for measuring the distance or position of an object in various industrial applications. Contact type sensors require physical contact with the object being measured, while non-contact type sensors can measure distance or position without touching the object. Contact type sensors are typically more affordable and ideal for simple applications, while non-contact type sensors offer higher accuracy and are often used in more complex or delicate settings. Both types play a crucial role in industrial automation and control systems.</p></p>
