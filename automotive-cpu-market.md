<p><strong>The Automotive CPU Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Microprogram Control Type</li><li>Logical Hard Wiring Structure Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive CPU market includes two main types: Microprogram Control Type and Logical Hard Wiring Structure Type. In Microprogram Control Type, the control unit executes instructions stored in microprograms, offering flexibility for updates and customization. On the other hand, Logical Hard Wiring Structure Type uses fixed logic circuits for instruction execution, providing faster processing speeds but less flexibility for changes. Both types have their advantages and are used in various automotive applications based on their specific requirements for performance, flexibility, and customization.</p></p>
